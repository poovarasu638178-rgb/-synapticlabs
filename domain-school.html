Questions

Frequently asked
by school students

Everything you need to know before ordering.

Is this only for Class 12 or all classes? +
We work with students from Class 6 all the way to Class 12. We also do tuition center and school exhibition projects. Just tell us your class and requirement.
Will my teacher know someone else built it? +
We build the model but you understand how it works. We always include an explanation card so you can explain the project yourself. Teachers will be impressed by how well you know it.
How much time do you need? +
Simple models take 2–3 days. Electronic projects take 4–7 days. For urgent orders, WhatsApp us and we'll try our best to meet your deadline.
What if the project stops working? +
We guarantee every project before delivery. If anything stops working within the delivery guarantee period, we fix or replace it — free of charge.
Can I see samples or ideas first? +
Yes! WhatsApp us and we'll share samples, photos of past projects, and suggest the best project for your subject and budget.
