Questions

Medical & research
student FAQs

We're MBBS / Pharmacy students — do you build for us? +
Absolutely. We build for MBBS interns doing their thesis, B.Pharmacy and M.Pharmacy students needing lab instruments, Biotech students needing equipment prototypes, and medical researchers needing custom devices. We understand that's a different requirement from engineering projects.
Can the device achieve clinical-grade accuracy? +
For research prototypes, we achieve validated accuracy using calibrated reference instruments. We include a validation report showing measured vs. reference values across test conditions.
Will you help with ethics committee documentation? +
Yes — for research-level projects, we help draft the technical sections of ethics submissions including device specifications, safety measures and intended use documentation.
Can you build a multi-parameter monitor? +
Yes. We've built systems measuring ECG, SpO2, temperature, respiration rate and blood pressure simultaneously — with wireless transmission and dashboard display. Talk to us about your parameters.
Do you supply components or do we have to? +
We handle all component sourcing — medical-grade sensors, enclosures, PCBs and everything else. You only need to give us your requirements and deadline.
